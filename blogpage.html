The Medical Hero Of Mogadishu

The story of Dr. Hashim Mohamed Farah is that of struggle, perseverance, fortitude and honesty. On Friday 9 July, it came as no surprise learning that Mogadishu’s Madina Hospital was declared the best medical facility in Somalia for 2021. The award was presented during a ceremony in Instabul Turkey. Dr. Hashim is the Hospital Director and has been at the helm of Madina Hospital from August 2020. I can vouch his astute management is responsible for the excellent services being provided at the hospital. As a seasoned journalist reporting on events in Mogadishu, I have on several occasions interacted with Dr. Hashim, particularly when the hospital is handling emergency cases.

This Somali Medical Surgeon is ever on call during traumatic emergencies working through long shifts without much rest. And in between his duties, Dr. Hashim never fails to make time and to answer my questions.From my experience as a journalist, being a doctor in Mogadishu is nerve wracking.

They see it all. I have heard of cases where gunmen demand to be given priority care. At times, medical staff are overwhelmed by the large number of casualties from terror attacks or internal conflicts.

In all these, our medical personnel always remain neutral and serve everyone notwithstanding from which clan, faction or political affiliation. Madina’s award as the best hospital in Somalia is vindication of Dr. Hashim’s dedicated leadership and service in saving lives. Dr. Hashim remains a Medical Hero for all Mogadishu residents.
